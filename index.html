About Me

Get to know my background, skills, and professional journey

Bioinformatics and Data Analyst with a Passion for Insights

Niguse Kelile Lema is a Bioinformatics and Data analyst at the Bio and Emerging Technology Institute. With a strong foundation in biotechnology and bioinformatics, he integrates data science, bioinformatics, and genomics to solve real-world problems.

Niguse aspires to become a leader in data-driven health and biotechnology solutions. He has led impactful research on antimicrobial resistance using shotgun metagenomics and microbial safety of traditional fermented foods, utilizing PacBio sequencing, to bridge science and analytics and drive innovation and public health impact.

I'm passionate about continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends in data analytics. My goal is to leverage data to solve real-world problems and contribute to organizational success through insightful analysis.
