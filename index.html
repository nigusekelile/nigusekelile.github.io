About Me

Get to know my background, skills, and professional journey

Bioinformatics and Data Analyst

Niguse Kelile Lema is a Bioinformatics and Data analyst. With a strong foundation in biotechnology and bioinformatics, he integrates data science, bioinformatics, and genomics to solve real-world problems.

I am aspiring to become a leader in data-driven health and biotechnology solutions. He has led impactful research on antimicrobial resistance using shotgun metagenomics and microbial safety of traditional fermented foods, utilizing PacBio sequencing, to bridge science and analytics and drive innovation and public health impact.

I'm passionate about continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends in Bioinformatics and data analytics. My goal is to use data to solve real-world problems and contribute to making the project successful through insightful analysis.
